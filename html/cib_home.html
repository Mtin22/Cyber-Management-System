DWK Introduction -


DWK plc, a renowned global financial institution with a rich history, boasts a workforce of 250,000 spread across numerous offices worldwide. Among its pivotal operational centers, the southern hub has organically grown into a pivotal strategic focal point. Here, various critical functions such as Operations, Technology, Client Services, and Corporate Groups converge to oversee the seamless functioning of daily operations and global financial systems spanning 75 markets globally.

At the heart of this hub are 6,000 dedicated professionals, including specialized teams catering to diverse needs across DWK plc's business spectrum. These teams encompass Human Resources, Global Real Estate, Application Development, Production Management, IT Support, Firmwide Risk Reporting, and the Major Incident Management (MIM) team.

Collaborating closely with DWK plc's network of strategic hubs worldwide, the MIM team ensures uninterrupted support across all time zones, ensuring round-the-clock service to meet client demands 365 days a year. Each hub offers a dynamic, high-performance work environment equipped with cutting-edge technology and top-notch amenities to support its staff effectively.
